Pet Health & Preventive Care

Keeping your pet healthy through preventive care, regular checkups, and early problem detection.

Vaccination Schedule

🐕 Core Dog Vaccines

  • 6-8 weeks: DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parainfluenza, Parvovirus)
  • 10-12 weeks: DHPP booster, Bordetella
  • 14-16 weeks: DHPP booster, Rabies
  • 12-16 months: DHPP, Rabies boosters
  • Every 1-3 years: Boosters as recommended

🐈 Core Cat Vaccines

  • 6-8 weeks: FVRCP (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, Panleukopenia)
  • 10-12 weeks: FVRCP booster
  • 14-16 weeks: FVRCP booster, Rabies
  • 12-16 months: FVRCP, Rabies boosters
  • Every 1-3 years: Boosters as recommended

Common Health Conditions

🦴 Arthritis

Common in: Senior pets, large breeds

Symptoms: Limping, stiffness, reluctance to move
Prevention: Weight management, joint supplements, regular exercise

🤧 Allergies

Common in: All breeds, seasonal

Symptoms: Itching, skin redness, ear infections
Prevention: Regular bathing, hypoallergenic diet, flea control

🦷 Dental Disease

Common in: Small breeds, seniors

Symptoms: Bad breath, difficulty eating, red gums
Prevention: Regular brushing, dental chews, professional cleanings

⚖️ Obesity

Common in: Indoor pets, certain breeds

Symptoms: Excess weight, difficulty breathing, lethargy
Prevention: Portion control, regular exercise, healthy treats

Pet First Aid Kit Essentials

  • Gauze pads and rolls
  • Adhesive tape
  • Antiseptic wipes
  • Hydrogen peroxide (3%)
  • Digital thermometer
  • Tweezers
  • Scissors with blunt ends
  • Disposable gloves
  • Saline solution
  • Pet-safe pain relievers
  • Emergency vet contact numbers
  • Blanket or towel

When to Visit the Vet

🚨 Emergency Situations

  • Difficulty breathing
  • Seizures or collapse
  • Severe bleeding
  • Ingestion of toxins
  • Unable to urinate
  • Eye injuries

📅 Schedule Appointment

  • Vomiting or diarrhea lasting >24 hours
  • Not eating for 24+ hours
  • Lethargy lasting 2+ days
  • Skin problems or itching
  • Behavior changes
  • Weight loss/gain
