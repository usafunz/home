Complete Cat Care Guide

Understanding your feline friend's unique needs for a happy, healthy life together.

Understanding Cat Behavior

Happy Signs: Purring, slow blinking, upright tail with curved tip

Warning Signs: Flattened ears, twitching tail, dilated pupils

üè† Territory & Space

Cats need vertical space, hiding spots, and consistent routines. Provide multiple resting areas and scratching posts.

Learn to interpret different meows, purrs, and chirps. Each cat has unique vocalizations for different needs.

Essential Cat Care Checklist

Daily Care Requirements

  • Fresh water changed daily
  • High-quality cat food (2-3 small meals)
  • Clean litter box (scoop 1-2 times daily)
  • Play and interaction time
  • Check for any health concerns
  • Grooming (brushing for long-haired cats)
  • Environmental enrichment
  • Fresh air access (if safe)

Indoor vs Outdoor Cats

üè° Indoor Cats

Lifespan: 12-20 years

Benefits: Safer from predators, diseases, and accidents

Requirements: Plenty of toys, scratching posts, window perches, and regular play

üå≥ Outdoor Cats

Lifespan: 2-5 years (average)

Benefits: Natural behavior expression, more exercise

Requirements: Regular vaccinations, flea/tick prevention, secure outdoor space
